Experiential websites are sites that are more than transactional. They don’t feature typical navigational features like menus and homepages. Instead, visitors have to explore the sites by scrolling, hovering, using the arrow keys on their keyboards and through the use of avatars. Experiential sites feature more than the standard text, photography and video content found on conventional sites. Many feature animations and 3D effects, lots have their own soundtracks, some come with narrators and all of them are interactive and immersive.
